Our Recycling Process

How Things Usually Work

Today usually parts are sent all over the world, to different suppliers and factories. There is no respect to quantity per transport, nor how it's transported. Environment is not considered.

What We Do

Collect all in one place. Some are recycled already in first stop, others are sent to a bigger factory for recycling. Quantity per transport is carefully considered for minimum impact on environment.

Our Contribution

Every act counts. Our Planet is in danger. Even if we only account for 0,000001% less impact, it counts. For each package sent to and/or from us we donate to replant at least one tree, often more.
